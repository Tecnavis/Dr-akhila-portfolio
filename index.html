Mrs. Akhilu Thomas, a dedicated counselling psychologist in Kochi, Kerala, holds extensive expertise in couple counselling, premarital counselling, and adolescent issues, boasting a decade of experience. Her personalized approach effectively addresses diverse mental health concerns such as anxiety, depression, stress, trauma, and relationship struggles. Passionate about enhancing mental well-being, she fosters a compassionate, non-judgmental environment for individuals to open up and discover their true value. Mrs. Thomas empowers clients to gain insights into their thoughts, emotions, and behaviors while developing coping strategies for improved mental wellness.
